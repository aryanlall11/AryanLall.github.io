    PROJECTS

    Project | 01
    Sensitivity analysis of energy production in PV power plants
    ​

    I performed a sensitivity analysis to understand the dependence of energy production on various parameters including temperature, Short-circuit current, Series resistance, Shunt-resistance, etc. and identify those parameters on which energy production is most sensitive.

    In order to calculate the plane of array irradiance for a location, I used the typical meteorological year data (TMY). This data was also used to obtain the module temperature at every hour using Sandia model and hence power using single diode model for PV module.

     

    These parameters could then be appropriately tuned and analyzed to obtain maximum throughput from PV modules

    Project | 02
    Ultra-fast Current-Voltage curve scanner
    Summer Industrial project 2019
    ​

    I designed a current-voltage (I-V) tracer circuit for power semiconductor devices using pulsed I-V measurement technique and Arduino mega 2560 as a key development board. This included optimizing the code and improving upon the standard circuit to achieve a minimum pulse width of 56μs with current measuring capability till 20A. The analog reading time of the Arduino is also reduced by 75%. PCB for the circuit is also designed keeping in mind the testing environment and user-friendly interface.

    ​

    This module would be deployed for accelerated testing of solar panels, monitoring the healthof by-pass diodes, in

    manufacturing lines and as testing measurement equipment for various purposes

    Project | 04
    Smart Paper cutter
    7th Inter IIT Tech meet 2018
    ​

    We designed a cheap and environment friendly cutter, that could segregate unused portion of an used paper, to be compiled into reusable format such as small pads or notebooks. This machine is capable of saving Rs.54,000 per year, by reusing old papers with a peak power consumption of 12W.

    ​

    I implemented image-processing technique including fast fourier transform for ruled pages, to recognize the unused portion of an already used paper with the help of contours and draw margins where it is supposed to be cut

    Our team won bronze medal in the campus sustainability challenge event out of 23 IIT’s who participated, where IIT Bombay emerged as 1st runner-up
